Project Context

The current blue slip system in APC is done manually, from the writing of credentials of 
the students to the delivering of the blue slips to the DO.

The developers’ proposed system is an automated blue slip issuance system that, on 
the security guard’s side, will utilize the existing Quick Response (QR) codes on the 
students’ IDs in getting the needed credentials by scanning, and a camera to detect and 
capture the dress code violation of the student, which is submitted through the system 
for reviewing and processing of the Discipline Office Head.
